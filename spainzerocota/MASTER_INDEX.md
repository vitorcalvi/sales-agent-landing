## Madrid Path Summary

### Year 1 Stack: ~13.000 €

| Program | Amount | Type | When |
|---------|--------|------|------|
| **Fomento del Empleo** | Up to 10.000 € | Grant | Year 1 |
| **Cuota Cero** | ~1.200 € | 100% Reimbursement | Months 1-12 |
| **Tarifa Plana** | ~1.200 € | Subsidy | Year 1 |
| **Kit Digital** | 3.000 € | Voucher | Anytime |

### Key Advantages
- ✅ Highest Year 1 cash grants
- ✅ Cuota Cero = 100% social security reimbursement
- ✅ Fomento up to 10.000 € for expenses
- ✅ No 3-month empadronamiento wait

### Key Disadvantages
- ❌ No rent caps (free market)
- ❌ Less lifestyle appeal (landlocked)

### Madrid Execution Path

```

Month 2:     Apply Kit Digital (3k €)
Month 2-12:  Collect Cuota Cero receipts for reimbursement
Month 12+:   Assess first hire, TU+1 equivalent
```

## Decision Framework

### Choose Madrid If:
- ✅ Maximum Year 1 cash is priority
- ✅ You can sign rental contract quickly
- ✅ Business/finance focus (not deep tech R&D)
- ✅ You don't mind landlocked city
- ✅ Faster start (no 3-month wait)

### Choose Barcelona If:
- ✅ Lifestyle/coastal access matters
- ✅ Long-term tech startup trajectory
- ✅ You value rent stability (zona tensionada)
- ✅ Can absorb ~6.000 € Year 1 gap
- ✅ Want ACCIÓ/Catalonia ecosystem access

## Long-Term Scaling Path (Both Cities)

### Phase 1: Autónomo (Year 1)
- City-specific grants (see above)
- Kit Digital (3k € nationwide)
- Tarifa Plana (nationwide)
- IRPF 7% (professional activities)

### Phase 2: First Hire (Year 1-2)
- Madrid: Contratación bonuses (~10k €)
- Barcelona: TU+1 (21.762 €) + Crea Feina (6-15k €)
- Both: Social security reductions

### Phase 3: Incorporation (Year 2+)
- Startup Law certification (free, enables 15% tax)
- ENISA Emprendedores (25k-300k € participative loan)

### Phase 4: Tech Scaling (Year 2-3)
- CDTI NEOTEC (up to 250k € grant, if own tech)
- ACCIÓ R&D grants (40% subsidy, Catalonia only)
- ENISA Crecimiento (up to 1.5M €)

### Phase 5: Major Investment (Year 3+)
- Fond-ICO Next Tech (1M-20M € equity)
- EIC Accelerator (up to 15M € EU grant)
- Horizon Europe (R&D consortium)

**Full Details**: See `ENISA_CDTI_FONDICO_REFERENCE_2026.md`

### Madrid
- ✅ No mandatory wait period
- ⚠️ Fomento del Empleo: Apply within first year of alta

### Barcelona
