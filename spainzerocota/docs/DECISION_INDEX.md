## Madrid Path Summary

### Year 1 Stack: ~13.000 €

| Program | Amount | Type | When |
|---------|--------|------|------|
| **Fomento del Empleo** | Up to 10.000 € | Grant | Year 1 |
| **Cuota Cero** | ~1.200 € | 100% Reimbursement | Months 1-12 |
| **Tarifa Plana** | ~1.200 € | Subsidy | Year 1 |
| **Kit Digital** | 3.000 € | Voucher | Anytime |

### Advantages
- ✅ Highest Year 1 cash grants
- ✅ Cuota Cero = 100% social security reimbursement
- ✅ Fomento up to 10.000 € for expenses
- ✅ No 3-month empadronamiento wait

### Disadvantages
- ❌ No rent caps (free market)
- ❌ Less lifestyle appeal (landlocked)

### Execution Path
```
Week 1:      Empadronamiento Madrid
Week 2:      Alta Hacienda + RETA
Week 2-4:    Apply Fomento del Empleo (10k €)
Month 2:     Apply Kit Digital (3k €)
Month 2-12:  Collect Cuota Cero receipts for reimbursement
Month 12+:   Assess first hire, TU+1 equivalent
```

| Program | Amount | Type | When |
|---------|--------|------|------|

| **Tarifa Plana REDUCIDA** | ~1.200 € | Subsidy | Year 1 |

### Advantages
- ✅ Rent caps in zona tensionada (entire city)
- ✅ Mediterranean lifestyle

- ✅ Equal long-term startup scaling potential

### Disadvantages
- ❌ ~6.000 € less Year 1 cash than Madrid

### Execution Path

Month 4-12:  Build client base, complete training
Month 12+:   Assess first hire, TU+1 (21.762 €)
Year 2-3:    ENISA, CDTI NEOTEC if scaling
