## STEP 1: ALTA HACIENDA (Tax Registration)

### 📍 URL
**https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es**

### 🔑 Login Method
Cl@ve (you already have this)

### 📝 Form
**Modelo 036** (Declaración censal de inicio de actividad)

### 🖱️ Click Sequence
1. Go to URL above
2. Click **"Acceso con Cl@ve"**
3. Enter your DNI/NIE and Cl@ve PIN
4. Navigate to **"Modelos y Trámites"** → **"Modelo 036/037"**
5. Select **"Alta"** (new registration)
6. Fill the form with the following:

### 📊 Form Fields

| Field | Value | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Epígrafe IAE - Primary** | **843.1** | Servicios de informática (software dev) |
| **Epígrafe IAE - Secondary** | **862.2** | Operaciones de comercio por cuenta propia (day trading) |
| **CNAE** | **6201** | Programación informática |
| **Domicilio Fiscal** | Your Barcelona address | Must match rental contract |
| **Régimen IVA** | Régimen general | Standard VAT |
| **Régimen IRPF** | Estimación directa simplificada | Simplified direct estimation |
| **Fecha de inicio** | **TODAY or when activity started** | ⚠️ REMEMBER THIS DATE! |

### 📄 Output to Save
**PDF Justificante de alta censal** - SAVE THIS FILE!

### ✅ Verification
- You'll receive email confirmation
- Download the PDF receipt
- The alta is effective immediately

## STEP 2: ALTA RETA (Social Security Registration)

### 📍 URL
**https://importass.seg-social.es**

### 🔑 Login Method

**The fecha de inicio in RETA MUST MATCH the fecha de inicio in Modelo 036 EXACTLY!**

## STEP 5: QUARTERLY TAX FILINGS

### 📍 URL
**https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es**

### 📅 Filing Calendar

| Quarter | Period | Deadline | Forms to File |
|---------|--------|----------|---------------|
| **Q1** | Jan-Mar | April 1-20 | 303 (IVA) + 130 (IRPF) |
| **Q2** | Apr-Jun | July 1-20 | 303 + 130 |
| **Q3** | Jul-Sep | October 1-20 | 303 + 130 |
| **Q4** | Oct-Dec | January 1-30 | 303 + 130 + 390 (Annual) |

### 📊 Form 303 (IVA/VAT)

**What to report:**
- Total invoices issued (with VAT)
- Total expenses (with VAT)
- VAT to pay or refund

### 📊 Form 130 (IRPF/Income Tax Advance)

**What to report:**
- Total revenue
- Allowable expenses
- IRPF to pay (advance on annual tax)

### 💡 Pro Tip
Configure **7% IRPF withholding** on your invoices (instead of 15%) for first 3 years as a professional autónomo. This gives you better cashflow!
