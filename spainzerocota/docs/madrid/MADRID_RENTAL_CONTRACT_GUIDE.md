## Essential Elements

Every rental contract in Madrid MUST include the following elements:

### 1. Parties Identification
- **Landlord (Arrendador)**: Full name, ID (DNI/NIE), address, contact details
- **Tenant (Arrendatario)**: Full name, ID (DNI/NIE/Passport), address, contact details

### 2. Property Details
- Full address
- Cadastral reference (referencia catastral)
- Description (size, rooms, features)
- Amenities (garage, storage room, furniture)
- Energy efficiency certificate (certificado de eficiencia energética)

### 3. Contract Duration
- **Minimum**: 1 year (contrato de temporada)
- **Housing contract**: 5 years (individual landlord) or 7 years (company)
- **Start date** and end date

### 4. Rent (Renta)
- Monthly amount in euros
- Payment method (bank transfer typically)
- Due date (usually first 5 days of each month)
- Rent update clause (actualización de renta)

### 5. Security Deposit (Fianza)
- **Legal minimum**: 1 month rent (residential)
- **Additional guarantee**: Up to 2 months (optional)
- Deposit return conditions

### 6. Utilities and Expenses
- Water (agua)
- Electricity (luz)
- Gas (gas)
- Internet/Telephone
- Community fees (gastos de comunidad)
- IBI (property tax)

### 7. Guarantees
- Bank guarantee (aval bancario)
- Guarantor (fiador)

## Legal Requirements (LAU)

The rental market in Spain is regulated by **Ley 29/1994 de Arrendamientos Urbanos (LAU)** and **Ley 12/2023 (Ley de Vivienda)**.

### Key Legal Points (2024-2026)

| Aspect | Rule |
|--------|------|
| **Minimum Duration** | 5 years (individual) / 7 years (company) |
| **Automatic Extension** | 3 years if neither party gives notice |
| **Rent Increase Cap** | Max 3% in 2024-2025 (IPC or new reference index) |
| **Deposit** | 1 month (must be deposited with IVIMA in Madrid) |
| **Early Termination** | Tenant: after 6 months with 30 days notice |
| **Landlord Notice** | 4 months before contract end |
| **Tenant Extension Right** | Up to 5 years total |

### Zonas de Mercado Residencial Tensionado

Madrid has designated "tensioned market areas" with special rules:
- Maximum rent caps may apply
- Previous contract rent must be disclosed
- Special termination conditions

## Key Clauses

### Recommended Clauses to Include

1. **Use of Property**: Habitual residence only
2. **Pets**: Allowed/not allowed
3. **Subletting**: Prohibited or with consent
4. **Alterations**: Not allowed without permission
5. **Non-Payment Procedure**: Grace period and consequences
6. **Maintenance Responsibilities**: Landlord vs tenant duties
7. **Termination Conditions**: Early termination penalties
8. **Inventory**: Detailed list of furniture and condition
9. **Notice Period**: How to properly give notice

### Sample Clause: Rent Update

```
CLÁUSULA DE ACTUALIZACIÓN DE RENTA

La renta se actualizará anualmente conforme al índice de precios al consumo
(IPC) publicado por el Instituto Nacional de Estadística, o el índice que lo
sustituya. El porcentaje de actualización no podrá exceder del 3% anual.
```

## Termination Rules

### Tenant Rights

| Scenario | Notice Required | Conditions |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| After 6 months | 30 days | Written notice |
| Before 6 months | Not allowed | Unless agreed with landlord |
| Contract end | 30 days | Must vacate |

### Landlord Rights

| Scenario | Notice Required | Conditions |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Contract end | 4 months | Must prove intent to occupy |
| Non-payment | Immediate | After 1 month unpaid |
| Unauthorized use | Varies | Case by case |
