## CRITICAL DISTINCTION: CASH GRANT VS. LOAN VS. VOUCHER

| Type | You Receive | Must Pay Back | When | Risk |
|------|-------------|----------------|------|------|
| **Cash Grant** | Direct money | NO | Immediate | Lowest |
| **Voucher** | Pays provider | NO | On service | Lowest |
| **Loan** | Direct money | YES | 1-2 months | Medium |
| **Equity** | Direct money | NO (ownership loss) | Negotiated | Highest |

**Your Goal**: MAXIMIZE cash grants + favorable loans + equity while MINIMIZING out-of-pocket.

## PROGRAM 2: STARTUP LOANS - DIRECT CASH, REPAYABLE

### ENISA Emprendedores - 25,000 to 300,000 €

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Admin** | ENISA (Empresa Nacional de Innovación) |
| **Type** | Participative loan (préstamo participativo) - DIRECT CASH |
| **Amount** | 25,000 € - 300,000 € (can combine lines up to 1.5M €) |
| **Interest** | Euribor + 4% to +6% (below market rates) |
| **Term** | 7 years, up to 5 years carencia (grace period, interest-only) |
| **Own Funds Required** | **1:1 match** (you put in 25k, ENISA lends 25k) |
| **Payment Timing** | Funds within 3 months of approval |
| **Total Direct Cash** | Up to 300,000 € (repayable) |
| **Eligibility** | - Company ≤ 24 months old<br>- Innovative business model<br>- Scalable project<br>- Spanish or EU company |
| **Application** | Year-round (no deadline) |
| **URL** | https://portaldelcliente.enisa.es:8443 |
| **Process Time** | 6-12 weeks |

**What "Participative" Means**:
- Subordinated debt: Paid after other creditors if bankruptcy
- No collateral required
- ENISA doesn't take equity or board seat
- Interest varies with company performance (profit sharing)
- Can convert to equity if agreed

**Advantage vs. Bank Loan**: No collateral, below-market rate, mentorship included.

## PROGRAM 3: TECH STARTUP GRANTS - DIRECT CASH (NON-REPAYABLE)

### CDTI NEOTEC - Up to 250,000 €

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Admin** | CDTI (Centro para el Desarrollo Tecnológico e Innovación) |
| **Type** | **GRANT** (subvención a fondo perdido) - NON-REPAYABLE |
| **Amount** | Up to 250,000 € (325,000 € with PhD contract) |
| **Coverage** | 85% of eligible project budget |
| **Minimum Budget** | 175,000 € |
| **Advance Available** | Up to 60% (max 150,000 €) WITHOUT guarantees |
| **Payment Timing** | Staged: 60% advance + 25% on delivery + 15% on completion |
| **Total Direct Cash** | Up to 212,500 € (85% of 250k) - NO REPAYMENT |
| **Success Rate** | Competitive (79-83.5 points cutoff in 2024) |
| **Timeline 2026** | - Opens: March 2026<br>- Deadline: April 2026<br>- Resolution: Sept-Nov 2026 |
| **URL** | https://www.cdti.gob.es | https://programa-neotec.es |
| **Eligibility** | - 6 months to 3 years old<br>- Technology developed IN-HOUSE<br>- Minimum 20k capital social fully disbursed<br>- NO dividends since constitution |

**CRITICAL**: NEOTEC is for **TECHNOLOGY DEVELOPMENT**, not tech services. If you develop proprietary software/tech (not just consulting), this is GOLD.

**Eligible vs. Ineligible**:
- ✅ Eligible: R&D staff salaries, lab equipment, external R&D, patents
- ❌ Ineligible: Sales, marketing, general admin, office rent
