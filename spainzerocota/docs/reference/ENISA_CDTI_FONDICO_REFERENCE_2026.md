## Section 1: ENISA Emprendedores (Startups y Pymes)

### 1.1 Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Administrator** | ENISA (Empresa Nacional de Innovación) |
| **Type** | Participative loan (préstamo participativo) |
| **Amount Range** | 25.000 € - 300.000 € |
| **Combined FEPYME** | Up to 1.500.000 € |
| **Application** | Year-round (no deadline) |
| **Success Rate** | ~33% direct, ~89% with professional preparation |

**Official Portal**: https://portaldelcliente.enisa.es:8443

### 1.2 Requirements

#### Mandatory Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Company Age** | Maximum 24 months from constitution |
| **Legal Form** | SL, SA, or similar (not sole trader/autónomo) |
| **Domicile** | Spain (or EU with significant Spanish presence) |
| **Business Model** | Innovative with competitive advantages |
| **Own Funds** | **1:1 match required** (loan = own funds minimum) |
| **Accounts** | Last fiscal year deposited in Registro Mercantil |
| **Financial Structure** | Balanced, professional management |
| **Viability** | Proven technical and economic viability |

#### Excluded Sectors
- Real estate (promoción inmobiliaria)
- Financial services (banca, seguros)
- Significant environmental harm activities

### 1.3 Financial Conditions

| Term | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| **Interest - Tranche 1** | Euribor + 4% to +6% (based on ENISA rating) |
| **Interest - Tranche 2** | 4.5% to 6.5% (variable, based on profitability) |
| **Opening Fee** | 0.5% of loan amount |
| **Amortization** | Up to 7 years |
| **Carencia (Grace)** | Up to 5 years (interest-only period) |
| **Payments** | Quarterly (interest + principal) |
| **Personal Guarantees** | **NONE required** |
| **Collateral** | **NONE required** |

### 1.4 What "Participative Loan" Means

- **Subordinated debt**: Paid after other creditors if liquidation
- **Profit participation**: Interest varies with company performance
- **No equity dilution**: ENISA doesn't take shares
- **No board seat**: ENISA is passive lender
- **Convertible**: Can convert to equity if agreed

### 1.5 Application Process

#### Step 1: Preparation (2-4 weeks)

**Required Documents**:
- [ ] Business plan (plan de empresa)
- [ ] Financial projections (3-5 years)
- [ ] Last fiscal year accounts (if applicable)
- [ ] CIF and escritura de constitución
- [ ] DNI/NIE of administrators
- [ ] Own funds justification (bank statements, capital social)

**Business Plan Must Include**:
1. Executive summary
2. Business model description
3. Market analysis
4. Competitive advantage (innovación)
5. Team background
6. Financial projections (P&L, cash flow, balance)
7. Use of funds

#### Step 2: Online Submission

1. Register at https://portaldelcliente.enisa.es:8443
2. Complete online questionnaire
3. Upload all documentation
4. Submit application

#### Step 3: Analysis Phase (6-12 weeks)

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1-2 | Documentation review |
| 3-4 | Financial analysis |
| 5-6 | Business model evaluation |
| 7-8 | Risk assessment |
| 9-10 | Investment committee review |
| 11-12 | Resolution |

#### Step 4: Formalization (if approved)

1. Receive approval letter (carta de aprobación)
2. Sign loan contract
3. Notary formalization (optional)
4. Funds transfer (within 3 months of approval)

### 1.6 Tips for Approval

**Do**:
- ✅ Demonstrate clear innovation/competitive advantage
- ✅ Show balanced financial structure
- ✅ Provide detailed financial projections
- ✅ Justify 1:1 own funds clearly
- ✅ Highlight team experience

**Don't**:
- ❌ Apply with insufficient own funds
- ❌ Submit incomplete documentation
- ❌ Over-project revenues (be realistic)
- ❌ Ignore ENISA feedback during analysis

## Section 3: CDTI NEOTEC 2026

### 3.1 Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Administrator** | CDTI (Centro para el Desarrollo Tecnológico e Innovación) |
| **Type** | Grant (subvención a fondo perdido) - **NON-REPAYABLE** |
| **Amount** | Up to 250.000 € (325.000 € with PhD contract) |
| **Coverage** | 85% of eligible budget |
| **Minimum Budget** | 175.000 € |
| **Advance Available** | Up to 60% (max 150.000 €) without guarantees |
| **Success Rate** | Very competitive (79-83.5 point cutoff in 2024) |

**Official Portal**: https://www.cdti.gob.es | https://programa-neotec.es

### 3.2 Timeline 2026

| Phase | Date |
|-------|------|
| **Convocatoria Opens** | March 2026 |
| **Application Deadline** | April 2026 |
| **Subsanación Period** | May 2026 |
| **Provisional Resolution** | September 2026 |
| **Definitive Resolution** | November 2026 |
| **Project Start** | January 2027 (earliest) |

### 3.3 Eligibility Requirements

#### Company Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | 6 months to 3 years from constitution |
| **Legal Form** | SME (pequeña empresa) |
| **Capital Social** | Minimum 20.000 € **fully disbursed** |
| **Dividends** | **NO dividends distributed** since constitution |
| **Listing** | NOT listed on stock exchange |
| **Origin** | No mergers, spin-offs, transformations |
| **Location** | Registered in Spain |

#### Technology Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Tech Development** | **OWNED/DEVELOPED IN-HOUSE** (not just services) |
| **R&D Intensity** | 10%+ of operating costs in R&D |
| **Innovation Level** | Substantially new products/processes/services |
| **Sector** | Technology-based (Deep Tech, Software, Biotech, etc.) |

**⚠️ Critical**: NEOTEC requires technology developed by YOUR company, not just tech services provided to clients.

### 3.4 Scoring System (100 points max)

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Technology** | 35 | Novelty, feasibility, IP protection |
| **Team** | 25 | Technical expertise, track record |
| **Market** | 20 | Size, growth, competitive advantage |
| **Business Model** | 10 | Scalability, revenue model |
| **Financial** | 10 | Viability, return potential |

**Minimum to qualify**: 50 points  
**Competitive threshold**: 79-83.5 points (2024 data)

### 3.5 Eligible vs Ineligible Costs

#### Eligible (85% funded)

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Personnel** | R&D staff salaries (up to 35k€/year/person) |
| **Equipment** | Lab equipment, hardware (depreciation) |
| **Subcontracting** | External R&D services (up to 30% of budget) |
| **Materials** | Consumables for R&D |
| **IP** | Patent costs, IP protection |

#### Ineligible

- Sales and marketing
- General administration
- Office rent (unless lab space)
- Travel and entertainment
- Capital social repayment

### 3.6 Application Process

#### Step 1: Preparation (2-3 months before)

**Technical Memory Must Include**:
1. Technology description and novelty
2. State of the art comparison
3. R&D methodology
4. Technical milestones and deliverables
5. Risk analysis
6. IP strategy

**Economic Memory Must Include**:
1. Detailed budget by category
2. Justification of costs
3. Timeline of expenses
4. Funding sources (own funds for 15% not covered)

#### Step 2: Online Submission

1. Register at CDTI portal
2. Complete online forms
3. Upload technical and economic memories
4. Submit before deadline

#### Step 3: Evaluation

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Documentation | 2 weeks | Completeness check |
| Expert Review | 6-8 weeks | External technical experts |
| Subsanación | 2 weeks | Address clarifications |
| Committee | 4 weeks | Investment committee decision |

#### Step 4: Resolution

1. Provisional resolution (September)
2. 10-day claim period
3. Definitive resolution (November)
4. Grant acceptance

### 3.7 Tips for NEOTEC Success

**Critical Success Factors**:
1. **Strong technical novelty**: Demonstrate clear innovation vs state of art
2. **Own technology**: Not services, but proprietary tech
3. **Qualified team**: PhD, engineers, relevant experience
4. **Clear IP strategy**: Patents, trade secrets planned
5. **Realistic budget**: 175k-250k range, well-justified
6. **Competent presentation**: Professional technical memory

**Common Rejection Reasons**:
- ❌ Technology not developed in-house
- ❌ Insufficient R&D component (just services)
- ❌ Weak innovation claim vs state of art
- ❌ Unrealistic projections
- ❌ Team lacks technical credentials
- ❌ Budget poorly justified

## Section 4: Fond-ICO Next Tech

### 4.1 Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Administrator** | ICO (Instituto de Crédito Oficial) + AXIS |
| **Total Fund** | 4.000.000.000 € |
| **Type** | Equity/quasi-equity investment |
| **Target** | Digital and AI sectors |
| **Leverage** | 2x private co-investment required |
| **Maximum Public Stake** | 49% |

**Official Portal**: https://www.ico.es/en/web/axis/fond-ico-next-tech  
**Contact**: info.next-tech@digital.gob.es

### 4.2 Investment Structure

#### Direct Company Investment

| Stage | Ticket Size | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **Startup** | Up to 1.000.000 € | Early traction required |
| **Scale-up** | Minimum 3.000.000 € | Series A+ |
| **Direct Range** | 5.000.000 - 20.000.000 € | Major rounds only |

#### Fund Investment (Venture Capital)

| Parameter | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| **Max per fund** | 100.000.000 € (public money) |
| **Max participation** | 49% of fund |
| **Leverage ratio** | 2:1 (private:public) |
| **Tech Transfer Vehicles** | Max 50.000.000 € |

### 4.3 How to Access

#### For Early-Stage Startups (<€1M ticket)

**Path**: Via VC funds that have Fond-ICO commitments

1. Identify VC funds with Fond-ICO Next Tech allocations
2. Apply to those funds following their process
3. If selected, Fond-ICO co-invests alongside private investors

**Key Insight**: You don't apply to Fond-ICO directly; you apply to VC funds that have Fond-ICO money.

#### For Scale-ups (>€3M ticket)

**Path**: Direct engagement with Fond-ICO/AXIS

1. Contact info.next-tech@digital.gob.es
2. Present investment opportunity
3. Demonstrate 2x private co-investment commitment
4. Due diligence and committee approval

### 4.4 Eligibility

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Location** | Social domicile in Spain |
| **Sector** | Digital, AI, Deep Tech |
| **Activity** | Significant operations in Spain |
| **Private Co-investment** | Majority private investors required |

### 4.5 Timeline

- **Application**: Rolling (no deadline)
- **Evaluation**: Per-deal basis (3-6 months)
- **Closing**: Standard VC round timeline

## Section 5: Startup Law Certification (Ley de Startups)

### 5.1 Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Official Name** | Ley 28/2022 (Ley de Fomento del Ecosistema de Empresas Emergentes) |
| **Effective** | December 23, 2022 |
| **Certifying Entity** | ENISA |
| **Cost** | FREE (certification is gratis) |
| **Validity** | Up to 5 years (7 for biotech, energy, industrial) |
| **Application** | Year-round |

**Official Portal**: https://portaldelcliente.enisa.es:8443

### 5.2 Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | Maximum 5 years (7 for biotech, energy, industrial) |
| **Legal** | No mergers, spin-offs, transformations since constitution |
| **Dividends** | NO dividends distributed |
| **Listing** | NOT listed on regulated market |
| **Domicile** | Spain (physical presence required) |
| **Staff** | At least 60% with employment contract in Spain |
| **Project** | Innovative and scalable entrepreneurship |
| **Revenue** | Maximum 10.000.000 € annually (any of 5 years) |
| **Compliance** | No significant environmental harm |

### 5.3 Benefits

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Corporate Tax** | 15% (vs 25% standard) for 4 years |
| **Stock Options** | Tax-exempt up to 50.000 €/year per employee |
| **Tax Deferrals** | 0% interest on postponed taxes |
| **SS Bonifications** | Up to 100 €/month for new hires |
| **R&D Deductions** | Enhanced (up to 59% R&D, 12% innovation) |
| **Investor Deduction** | 50% for angel investors |

### 5.4 Application Process

1. Register at ENISA portal
2. Complete certification questionnaire
3. Upload supporting documentation
4. Wait for evaluation (max 6 months)
5. Receive certification if approved

### 5.5 Documentation Required

- Escritura de constitución
- CIF
- Last annual accounts (if applicable)
- Innovation/scalability justification
- Staff location declaration

## Section 6: Strategic Application Sequence

### 6.1 Timeline for New Startup

```
Month 1-2:   Incorporate SL
Month 2-3:   Startup Law Certification application
Month 3-4:   ENISA Emprendedores application
Month 4-5:   NEOTEC application (if tech-developing company, <3 yr)
Month 6+:    ENISA Crecimiento (if >2 yr, revenue)
Year 3+:     Fond-ICO Next Tech (if scale-up, Series A+)
```

### 6.2 ENISA vs NEOTEC Decision Matrix

| Your Situation | Recommended First |
|----------------|-------------------|
| **Tech services company** | ENISA only (NEOTEC requires own tech) |
| **Proprietary tech, R&D intensive** | NEOTEC + ENISA parallel |
| **Need funding fast** | ENISA (year-round, faster) |
| **Want non-repayable grant** | NEOTEC (but competitive) |
| **<24 months old** | ENISA Emprendedores |
| **6mo-3yr, own tech** | NEOTEC in parallel |

### 6.3 Combined Stack Potential

| Stage | Programs | Total Potential |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| **Seed** | Startup Law + ENISA Emprendedores | ~300k € |
| **Early** | + NEOTEC | ~550k € (300k loan + 250k grant) |
| **Growth** | + ENISA Crecimiento | ~2.000.000 € |
| **Scale** | + Fond-ICO Next Tech | 5.000.000 €+ |
