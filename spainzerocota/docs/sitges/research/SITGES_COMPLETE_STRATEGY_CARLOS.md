### PHASE 2: REGISTRATION (Day 1 — The Grant Clock Starts)

**CRITICAL**: Many grants require RETA alta date within specific windows (e.g., 2024-2025 for Emprèn Cat). Choose timing strategically.

#### 2.1 Modelo 036/037 (AEAT Census)
**When**: Same day as RETA alta (or day before)

**How**:
- Online: sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es → "Modelo 036/037" → "Alta en el Censo de Empresarios"
- **Key fields**:
  - **CNAE codes**: 
    - 6201: Computer programming activities
    - 6419: Financial intermediation (for day trading) — OR use 6499 for other financial services
  - **IAE epígrafe**: Choose appropriate for your activities
  - **Fecha de inicio de actividad**: Choose strategically (consider grant deadlines)
  - **Domicilio fiscal**: **Sitges address** (your empadronamiento address) - MUST BE SITGES for local grants
  - **Régimen de IVA**: General regime (Régimen General) unless you qualify for other regimes
  - **Régimen de estimación**: Usually "Estimación directa" for developers

**Cost**: FREE

**Output**: "Justificante de presentación" (save PDF immediately)

---

#### 2.2 RETA Alta (Seguridad Social)
**When**: Same day as Modelo 036 (or next day)

**How**:
- Online: importass.seg-social.es → "Alta en trabajo autónomo"
- **Key fields**:
  - **Fecha de inicio de actividad**: MUST match Modelo 036 date exactly
  - **Base de cotización**: Minimum €960.78/month (for Tarifa Plana)
  - **SELECT TARIFA PLANA**: Checkbox for "Tarifa Plana de 80 euros mensuales" — this is automatic for new autónomos
  - **IBAN account**: Your EU IBAN (Spanish or any EU bank) ✅
  - **CNAE**: Same as Modelo 036

**Cost**: FREE

**Output**: "Resolución de alta en RETA" (download immediately — this is your grant clock proof)

**⚠️ CRITICAL**: The "Fecha de inicio de actividad" you choose may affect eligibility for certain programs that require activity start within specific date ranges.
